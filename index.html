Zica Zoe

I create music that blends Afrobeat, Pop, and Caribbean vibes—timeless melodies you’ll never forget. My sound is a journey of rhythm, passion, and storytelling, crafted to make you feel every beat and lyric. From heartfelt ballads to energetic anthems, my music speaks to love, life, and everything in between. Whether you're vibing to my latest EP, Not Easy To Forget, or discovering my sound for the first time, you’re in for an unforgettable experience.
